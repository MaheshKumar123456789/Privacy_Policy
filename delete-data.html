Request Data Deletion

At GoTrollys, we respect your right to privacy and control over your personal data. If you have used our application and would like your stored data to be permanently deleted, you may request it at any time.

How to Request Deletion

Please send an email from your registered email address to:

kommumaheshmahesh24@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request - [Your Name]

What Information Will Be Deleted?

Upon receiving your request, we will remove the following from our active databases:

  • Your personal profile (Name, Email, Phone Number).
  • Your ride and transport booking history.
  • Any stored location preferences.

Processing Time

Once we verify your identity, your information will be deleted from our systems within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.
